About Us

At Sunrays Generic Pharmacy Pvt Ltd, We are committed to improving health and well-being by providing trusted pharmaceutical products and personalized care to our communities. As a modern pharmacy retail chain we offer wide range of Branded, Generic, Ayurvedic, Surgical, Injectable and the Prescription medication, OTC medicine, healthcare essentials and wellness products - all delivered with accuracy, care and convenience.

Our licensed pharmacists and trained staff prioritize patient safety, expert guidance and friendly service. We combine professional healthcare standards with easy access, affordable pricing and reliable supply to ensure every customer receives the support they need for a healthier life.

With growing network of stores and a focus on innovation Sunrays Generic Pharmacy is dedicated to being your neighborhood pharmacy.
